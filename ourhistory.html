Brand History

The story of Cofy Coffee began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a coffee shop that not only serves premium coffee but also empowers the local community. Launched as a specialty coffee brand, Cofy Coffee quickly gained popularity for its high-quality Arabica Gayo coffee and a dedication to providing an affordable yet premium coffee experience. The brand’s name became synonymous with quality, innovation, and community spirit, appealing to a market of millennials and general coffee lovers who valued both taste and atmosphere.

Cofy Coffee's were marked by strategic growth, establishing 3 new stores across universities, hospitals, and office areas. The coffee shop brand didn’t just focus on expanding its footprint but also invested heavily in empowering young entrepreneurs and local coffee farmers. This community-driven approach fostered a loyal customer base and strong brand equity, particularly within university communities.

This coffee brand was born—a brand built on the core values of honesty, transparency, and community empowerment. While retaining the quality coffee and innovation was known for, Cofy Coffee introduced a more inclusive approach. This new brand expanded its offerings by combining the modern fresh brew coffee experience with the nostalgic flavors of traditional kopitiam-style coffee, creating a unique blend of old and new.

With a logo featuring a bird that symbolizes freedom and authenticity, Cofy Coffee positions itself as a brand that fosters a culture of truth and inclusivity. The focus remains on building relationships—not only with customers but also with coffee farmers, local roasteries, and aspiring entrepreneurs.

Cofy Coffee reflects a natural progression towards a brand that is more than just about coffee—it is about empowering the entire coffee supply chain, from the farmers to the consumers. With a mission to empower communities through every cup served, Cofy Coffee aims to be a brand that brings people together, one cup and one story at a time.
